Speech Samples

The model is evaluated with speech from two database:
English: VCC2018[1]
Mandarin: The Speech synthesis-library of average model provided by Databaker(http://www.data-baker.com).

There are 2 systems implemented for comparison:
iSE<\b>: the cross-lingual VC system using the i-vector based Speaker Embedding (iSE), and we benchmark this as our baseline.
SEN<\b>: the proposed jointly trained Speaker Embedding Network (SEN).
